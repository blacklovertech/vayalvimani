About Vayal Vimaani

Revolutionizing Indian agriculture through innovative drone technology

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Vayal Vimaani is revolutionizing Indian agriculture by bridging the gap between traditional farming and cutting-edge drone technology.

Our mission is to make precision agriculture accessible to every farmer, regardless of farm size or location. By connecting farmers with a network of certified drone service providers, we're helping to increase yields, reduce costs, and promote sustainable farming practices across India.

Our Vision

To become India's leading platform for smart farming solutions, empowering 1 million farmers with drone technology by 2030.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our drone fertilization services

Pricing typically ranges from ₹300-₹600 per acre depending on crop type, terrain, and service scope. We offer volume discounts for larger farms. Contact us for an exact quote based on your specific requirements.

Our services are effective for all major crops including paddy, wheat, cotton, sugarcane, maize, pulses, and horticultural crops. The technology is particularly beneficial for crops requiring precise nutrient management.

Drone fertilization offers several advantages: uniform coverage (no overlaps/misses), 5-10x faster application, 20-30% less chemical usage, no soil compaction, and ability to treat difficult terrain. It also provides digital records of applications.

All our operators are DGCA-certified and follow strict safety protocols. Drones maintain safe distances from people/animals, use approved chemicals at correct concentrations, and operate only in suitable weather conditions with proper insurance coverage.

We recommend booking at least 3-5 days in advance during peak seasons. For larger farms (50+ acres), 1-2 weeks notice is ideal to ensure availability. Emergency services may be available with additional charges.
